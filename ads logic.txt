Bilkul bhai 👍 main tumhari poori flow (Splash + Ads + Strings + Handler + Activities) sirf 10 lines me summary deta hoon:

strings.xml me tumne Google ke test IDs diye hain (App ID, Banner, Interstitial, Native). ✅

AndroidManifest.xml me App ID meta-data properly add hai (com.google.android.gms.ads.APPLICATION_ID). ✅

Ads.java ek helper class hai jo InterstitialAd load aur show handle karti hai.

loadAd() → ek AdRequest banata hai aur ad async load karta hai (success ya fail callbacks).

show() → agar ad loaded hai to display karega, warna onFailed() callback trigger karega.

SplashActivity app ka entry point hai jo pehle Ads.loadAd() call karta hai.

Handler ka use karke 4 sec ka fallback timer rakha hai (agar ad na dikhe to MainActivity).

Agar ad successfully show ho jaye → dismiss hote hi MainActivity open ho jati hai.

Agar ad fail ho ya timeout ho jaye → bhi MainActivity open ho jati hai.

MainActivity me agar user back press kare to fir se SplashActivity start ho jati hai.

👉 Matlab: Ad optional hai → load ho jaye to dikhega, warna app 4 sec ke andar guaranteed MainActivity me chala jayega.
